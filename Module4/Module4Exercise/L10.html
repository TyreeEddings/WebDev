Fun Facts - Tyree Eddings

Fun Fact #1: Three presidents, all Founding Fathers—John Adams, Thomas Jefferson, and James Monroe—died on July 4
Fun Fact #2: One of President John Tyler’s grandsons is still alive today—and he was born in 1790.
Fun Fact #3: The tallest man ever recorded was American giant Robert Wadlow (1918–1940), who stood 8 feet 11 inches.
Fun Fact #4: The tallest living man is 37-year-old Sultan Kosen, from Turkey, who is 8 feet, 2.8 inches.
Fun Fact #5: The first college football game was played on November 6, 1869, between Rutgers and Princeton
Fun Fact #6: The Four Corners is the only spot in the US where you can stand in four states at once: Utah, Colorado, Arizona and New Mexico.
