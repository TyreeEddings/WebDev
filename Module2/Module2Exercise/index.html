About BAWSI

The Bay Area Women's Sports Initiative (BAWSI) is a public benefit, nonpropfit corporation with a mission to create programs and partnerships through which women athleats bring health, hopw and wholeness to out community. Founded in 2005 by Olympic and World cup soccer stars Brandi Chastain and Julie Foudy and Marlene Bjorsrud, former general manager of the San Jose CyberRays women's professional soccer team, BAWSI provides a meaningful path for women athletes to become a more visivle and valued part of the Bay Area sports culture
